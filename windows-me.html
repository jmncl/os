Windows ME Installation

Windows ME

Windows ME revolutionized personal computing with its user-friendly graphical interface, the introduction of the Start menu, and built-in support for plug-and-play hardware. It marked a significant shift from the earlier MS-DOS-based systems, offering a more intuitive experience for home users. Windows 95 also introduced long filenames, improved multimedia capabilities, and integrated internet support, making it a landmark release that defined the future of consumer operating systems.
